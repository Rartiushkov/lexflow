The core loop, in minutes.

A protected workflow designed around how immigration cases actually move.

01
Create a case

The lawyer opens the dashboard and creates a case. LexFlow generates a secure, client-specific link.

02
Client uploads & pays

The client follows the checklist, pays the invoice via Stripe, and uploads passport scans and contracts.

03
AI parses & fills

The backend reads the documents, validates the data with Pydantic, and injects it into the official government PDF.

04
Lawyer submits

The final form is downloaded and submitted to the authority. Source files are purged automatically after the retention window.
